# User Guide

## Overview

Acid Rain is a platformer game where you must navigate through dangerous environments while avoiding periodic acid rain storms. Your goal is to reach the safe zone at the end of the level.

## Objective

Reach the end safe zone to complete the level.

## Gameplay

### Controls
- Use standard platformer controls (W/D and space) to move and jump through the level
- Use the left mouse button to attack enemies

### Enemies
- Enemies are positioned in shelter areas throughout the level
- Defeat enemies to progress safely
- Watch out for enemy attacks

### Acid Rain
- Acid rain occurs periodically during gameplay
- When acid rain is active, you take damage if you're exposed to the sky
- Take cover under shelter (roofs, platforms, or structures) to avoid damage
- A warning appears before the rain starts, giving you time to find shelter

### Health Packs
- Health packs are scattered throughout the level
- Walk over health packs to heal and restore your health
- Use health packs strategically after taking damage from enemies or acid rain

## Tips

- Pay attention to the acid rain warning to avoid getting caught in the open
- Use shelters both for protection from rain and as tactical positions against enemies
- Collect health packs when you need them, but remember their locations for later
- Plan your route to stay near shelter areas when possible
